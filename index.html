Projects

Memory Card Matching Game

A modern take on the classing matching card game. This browser based game features a star rating based on performance, a move counter, and a timer. To win simply match all cards on the board, then a pop up box will appear congratulating the player and showing them their stats from the game and giving them an option to restart.

Frogger Arcade Game

A classic recreation of the popular arcade game frogger. Features enemy characters that move dynamically through the map that the player must avoid. To win, the player must make it across the road to the water using the arrow keys without running into an enemy character. After you make it to the water the player is greeted with a pop up dialog congratulating them and telling them their stats for the game.
